## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities privately via [GitHub Security
Advisories](https://docs.github.com/code-security/security-advisories). Do not
open a public issue.

**Our response SLA:**

- **Acknowledgement:** within one business day.
- **Triage:** assigned to the on-call rotation.
- **Resolution:** followed by an incident retrospective, regardless of severity.

### Threat Model

Our threat model covers the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of each
service and its deployment pipeline. Identified risks are reviewed by the
Architecture Review Board and tracked to closure.
